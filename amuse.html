I love trying to find weird or funny websites and videos. I love the bizarre and unusual things though, so fair warning.

Do You Want to Slap Someone with a Eel?

Well now you can! This website allows us to slap a man back and forth with an eel. Fun fact: The word Cornobble means "to slap someone with a dead fish". Bet you did not know that! (I do not know why I do, though.)

Slap! That! Man!

Oh god, Chicken on a Raft

I shudder at the thought of this website. This website plays the song "Chicken on a Raft" for as log as you stay on the website. The goal is to endure it for as long as possible, no muting, just "Chicken on a Raft".
My old friend Lucas once managed to stay on that website for over 24 hours. I don't know how.
